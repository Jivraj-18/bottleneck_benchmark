# IN-2026-02-19-UPSIDA — Working of Uttar Pradesh State Industrial Development Authority (UPSIDA)

## Background
A state industrial development authority is responsible for planned industrial area development, infrastructure provision, plot allotment, and related control systems across a large portfolio of industrial areas.

## System context
- The authority manages many industrial areas across the state.
- Its mandate includes planning, land development, infrastructure, allotment, and internal controls.
- Targets were repeatedly missed across land acquisition, land development, allotment, and recoveries.
- Audit findings cut across both operational execution and institutional governance.

## Timeline
- 2017/18–2022/23: audit period for many performance findings.
- 2024: some government responses are noted in the audit materials.
- 2026-02-19: the report is tabled.

## Observations
- Perspective, development, redevelopment, and zonal plans were not finalized as expected.
- Land acquisition and development targets were repeatedly missed.
- Contracting and allotment processes showed multiple irregularities.
- Annual accounts were not prepared since inception, which affects oversight and merger/compliance matters.

## Task
Analyze this case. Identify the main binding constraint, the slow variables, the top 3 interventions, 2 things to deprioritize, and the earliest warning signals leadership should track.
